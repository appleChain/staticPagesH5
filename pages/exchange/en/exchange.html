What is Instant Purchase?

Instant Purchase is the ability to buy VITE (native VITE tokens) with ETH-000 (Issued on Vite Chain, by Vite Labs Official Gateway).


After the purchase, the following three steps will take place:

1. Vite Labs will exchange ETH-000 to ETH (on Ethereum chain);

2. Vite Labs will purchase VITE (ERC20) with said ETH;

3. Vite Labs will burn VITE (ERC20) of the same amount as your purchase.
