What is Quota?

The Vite network does not consume VITE like on Ethereum where you have to pay ETH as network fees. Instead, the Vite network consumes Quota.

There are two ways to get Quota:

1) Locking your VITE. All locked VITE can be redeemed after 3 days;

2)Proof of Work (POW) computation provided by Vite Labs.
